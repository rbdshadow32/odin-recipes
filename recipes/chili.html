Easy Homemade Chili

Description

Homemade chili is delicious and goes great with cornbread or over corn chips for a chili pie!

This homemade chili is loaded with beff, beans and crowd-pleasing flavor.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned goods
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and the onion.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and let simmer for 15 minutes.
